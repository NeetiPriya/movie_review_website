This is an review website for recommendation for movies, serials , series and short films for the users to let them know about the latest movies.

Online reviews help consumers make purchasing decisions. Review portal is a site that collect opinions from customers and publish them for others. The principle is based on bundling and checking reviews of several movies, short films , series and serials to provide a serious access to other users. Users can conclude to a decision to choose from the one to five stars or from a text or through story summary.
